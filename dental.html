DENTAL DEPARTMENT

Services

We at Vathiyayath Multispeciality Dental Clinic, the best dental clinic in Perumbavoor, serves you exceptional dental treatment at affordable rates. With the use of modern technology, Hi-tech facilities and professional skills, we offer you efficient and comfortable dental service. We make every effort to make your dental visit an easy and hassle-free experience.

Book an appointment with us, consult with the best dentist in Perumbavoor and get the solution for all your dental problems!

1.Orthodontic Treatment- braces, invisible braces

Are you worried about having irregularly aligned teeth? It's hard to give that beautiful smile if your teeth are showing different directions. Orthodontic treatment deals with diagnosis, prevention and correction of mal-positioned teeth and jaws, and misaligned bite patterns.
You can align your teeth in proper positions with the use of orthodontic braces.
We even have invisible braces if you are worried about the looks of having metal braces.
Consult us today to receive the best Orthodontic treatment in Perumbavoor, and get your beautifully aligned teeth!

2.Single sitting root canal treatment

If you are a busy bee, and can't spend a lot of time on the multiple root canal treatment, you are at the right place! Single sitting root canal treatment will both save your time and give you faster and efficient results.
A root canal treatment is usually done if there is inflammation or infection in the roots of your tooth. Root canal treatment removes infected tissues from your tooth followed by cleaning and filling the remaining space.
Are you looking forward to root canal treatment which is not a tedious process?
Consult us and get the best single sitting root canal treatment in Perumbavoor.

3.Tooth whitening/bleaching

Are you losing your confident smile because your teeth are yellow?
Your teeth enamel can get stained or discolored through various ways and it is a common concern.
Teeth whitening or bleaching is a one-time procedure of removing stains from the teeth's surface and restoring the natural color of your teeth.
It is a reliable and long-lasting treatment that you can afford at our dental clinic. We have the best teeth whitening treatment in Perumbavoor.
Consult us today and get your confident white teeth back!

4.Treatment for bleeding gums and loose tooth

Are you suffering from a loose tooth or bleeding gums?
A Tooth can lose support and slowly detach from gums and bones. The slightest touch, chewing or eating can easily move it or cause further loosening of the tooth. Although this is common in kids, adults can also experience this with bleeding gums or gum disease.
If you are experiencing this, don't worry, you are at the right place. We have the perfect treatment for such bleeding gums and loose teeth. We can easily reattach your tooth to gums and treat your bleeding gums with the best treatment possible!
Contact us now and learn more about this dental treatment from the best dental clinic in Perumbavoor.

5.Gum depigmentation

Do you want to change your darker looking gums?
Your gums can look darker or brown rather than pink if excess melanin builds up. This can also be due to smoking, side effects of certain medications, amalgam fillings on gum or any other reasons.
Whatever the reason may be, the bottom line is— you can get pink and healthy gums! Through Gum depigmentation/Gum bleaching you can easily get those beautiful gums.
What are you waiting for? Book an appointment with us and get your Gum depigmentation done from the best dentist in Perumbavoor.

6.Crowns- Metal free crowns, Metal ceramic crowns

What are dental crowns?
Dental crowns are caps placed over your tooth to restore the functionality of a tooth without the need for removing the bad tooth such as broken, excessively worn, or one destroyed by tooth decay.
If you are embarrassed of your broken or decayed tooth, but you are not willing to remove that tooth, go for dental crowns! With us, you get the best dental service and advice.
We have metal free crowns and metal ceramic crowns as well.
Contact us at the earliest and find out what's best for you.

7.Laser dentistry

What is laser dentistry? Dentists use lasers to treat a number of different dental conditions such as removing overgrown tissue, reshaping the gums, whitening teeth, or for other oral treatments.
Compared to drill and other non-laser tools, laser dentistry offers a more comfortable and cost effective treatment. Not only that, this innovative type of dental care gives you painless, faster and enhanced results. Everything you wish for! Our dental clinic gives the best laser dentistry in Perumbavoor.
Discuss your laser treatment option with our dentist today.

8.Dental Implants

Are you worried about your missing tooth? We have an option for you!
A dental implant is an artificial tooth root placed in missing tooth areas to support a dental prosthesis such as crown, denture, etc., without disturbing other teeth. You can go for this to replace your missing tooth or a group of missing teeth.
Dental implants will not only improve your look, but serve the same function of your normal teeth.
Do you need dental implants?
Contact us or book an appointment with us.
Why wait when you are sure to get the best dental implant fixing service in Perumbavoor?

9.Full/partial dentures

From now, no more worries or embarrassment for your missing teeth. With full or partial dentures you can smile again with confidence!
A denture or false teeth is a removable prosthetic appliance worn as replacement for missing teeth. Two types of dentures are available— full or complete denture and partial denture.
If all the teeth are missing, one can restore to full denture. If some natural teeth remain, one can fill the teeth gaps with partial denture.
If you are looking for full or partial dentures, restore your teeth appearance and function with us!

10.Invisalign

Invisalign is a type of clear aligner which is an orthodontic device that is a transparent, plastic form of dental braces used to align teeth. Invisalign is a good alternative for metal braces and is loved by kids, teens and adults alike.
If you don't like wearing metal braces, you should definitely try Invisalign. It is more comfortable, good looking and gives faster results than metal braces.
If you are interested in Invisalign, contact us today! We have the best Invisalign treatment in Perumbavoor. Resort to Invisalign and align your teeth quickly with us!

11.Cosmetic dentistry

Are you unhappy with the shape, color or texture of your teeth? Are you not able to give your beautiful smile?
You can have a beautiful, glowing smile with the help of cosmetic dentistry. It includes smile designing and smile correction. Cosmetic dentistry is all about improving your appearance. Its primary focus is on improving the color, size, shape, position and alignment of teeth/gums and by that, your overall appearance will turn amazing.
So, what's stopping you? We offer the best cosmetic dentistry in Perumbavoor.
Contact us today and get that amazing change in your appearance!

12.Smile designing

Importance of a beautiful smile is not new.
Smile designing is an artistic way of creating beautiful, whiter, straighter and natural smiles. You can fully restore your dental appearance despite the original state of your teeth.
Also as people age, there can be discoloration of teeth, the muscle's tonus wear which result in less tooth display, etc. It is a useful cosmetic dentistry for all ages.
Thought smile designing anyone can enhance their smile.
Consult us today and achieve an attractive, beautiful and natural smile!

13.Surgical/non surgical extraction

Do you need a tooth extraction?
Tooth extraction is a common need a person encounters at least once in a lifetime.
Dentists follow one of the two procedures for that– Surgical extraction and Non-surgical extraction. Surgical extraction is done for the teeth that are not visible in the mouth. Whereas, Non-surgical extraction is done for the teeth that are not hiding in the gums and are clearly visible.
For clear, precise and painless extraction of teeth, expert hands are needed.
Consult our experienced and skillful dentist and get the best Surgical/non-surgical extraction in Perumbavoor.

14.Preventive orthodontics (speciality for kids)

Preventive orthodontics avoids any appearance of abnormalities in the alignment of teeth at early stages in a child's life. It allows better control over the gaps created by the loss of first teeth in your kid.
There are dental appliances called "space holders" that can help the permanent teeth to grow correctly in children.
Controlling the alignment of teeth in your child as early as possible (2-14 years of age) is the best thing you could do!
Contact us at the earliest and get detailed information on Preventive Orthodontics. We assure your child beautifully aligned teeth!

15.Dental care for children

Giving your child good dental care at the earliest, is one of the best things you could do for them!
Milk teeth are the foundation for developing permanent teeth, that's why it is always being advised to take care of teeth from early childhood. Dental care for children include: teeth examination, treatments for tooth cavity and dental injuries, habit counseling, fluoride treatment, etc.
Being one of the best dental clinics in Perumbavoor, we work towards giving kids the best oral health possible!
Contact us at the earliest to give your kids good dental for life!

16.Dental digital X-ray

Are you worried about exposure and safety while taking dental X-rays?
Good news is, you don't have to. Dental digital X-rays is an advanced technology that emits low levels of radiation. It is efficient and safer than the traditional X-ray technology.
Dental X-ray is an important part of dental treatments as it is used for close inspections, identifying small cavities, etc.
Reduce your risk of exposure to traditional X-rays! Resort to the Dental digital X-ray we provide.
Consult us and receive the best dental digital X-ray treatment in Perumbavoor.

17.Dental veneers

What are Dental veneers?
Dental veneers are wafer-thin, tooth colored, custom-made shells of porcelain that are attached to the front surface of teeth, to recreate the natural look.
If you have broken, discolored or damaged teeth, Dental veneers are one of the best options. They have more resilience and strength when compared to natural tooth enamel. Moreover, it will enhance the appearance of your teeth!
Are you in need of Dental veneers?
Feel free to contact us if you have any queries.
Consult us today to get Dental veneers from the best dental clinic in Perumbavoor, at the earliest!
