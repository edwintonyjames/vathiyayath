About Us

"WE CARE GOD CURES"

VATHIYAYATH MAKKAR MEMORIAL HOSPITAL is one of Perumbavoor's most trusted healthcare providers over the last 14years. The Hospital is equipped to meet any type of medical emergencies with the support of competent professionals, state-of-the-art infrastructure modern equipments and advanced facilities including 150 beds and 20 ICU beds. Our Motto "WE CARE GOD CURES" sums up our ideals. Founded by late Eng.V.M MAKKAR in the year of January 2000. Eng. V.M Makkar, was chief Engineer in Tamil Nadu P W D and structural construction Department. He was a well talented and dynamic personality who had a long vision of future. Over the years the hospital has grown in stature, keeping a pace with the advancements in medical science and technologies. The hospital provides comprehensive healthcare services including general medical and surgical services as well as tending to the most challenging medical conditions. Comfortable, well equipped rooms and competent and caring medical and paramedic staff, facilities for patients companions etc. ensure utmost comfort to the patients. Each patient floor has a well-equipped nursing station manned by highly trained personnel and supervisory and housekeeping staff. Skilled Doctor's services and diagnostic facilities are available for the admitted patients round the clock. A caring environment prevails in the hospital with all our professionals working with quiet efficiency. The hospital has 24 hour casualty with a sophisticated intensive, accident care unit and it receives the status of a "mother and baby friendly hospital". In the hospital there are several departments like Casualty , Enquiry , Medical O.P, Gynecology, Orthopedics, General surgery, Pediatrics, ENT, Nephrology, Cardiology departments, Chest and Diabetic and Physiotherapy.
